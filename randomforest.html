The focus of this project is developing machine learning models that can accurately predict the price of a used vehicle based on its features, in order to make informed purchases. We implement and evaluate various learning methods on a dataset consisting of the sale prices of different makes and models across cities in the United States. Our results show that Random Forest model, but is compute heavy.
