OVERVIEW A risk factor is anything that increases chances of getting a disease, such as cancer. But having a risk factor, or even many, does not confirm to get the disease. While we cannot change some breast cancer risk factors like family history and aging, etc. While there are some factors that can be controlled. Thus diagnosing the cancer at the earliest stage is very important. Thus Deep Learning techniques like Convolutional Neural Networks are used widely for the prediction of the breast cancer risk. This prediction is done using Python Flask model, in which a web application is built to analyze the cancer risk.
